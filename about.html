About Me

Originally form the UK, I moved to Medicine Hat in 2019 with my now wife. Currently I am studying at Medicine Hat College and working towards an IT Diploma, Majoring in Software. I have so far attained a 4.0 GPA in my first semester, and I plan on continuing that trend throughout the rest of my time here.

Before becoming a student I worked at a company named Adapt Mobility, where I would regularly adapt solutions for clients with accessability needs. From installing grab bars in showers, to creating bespoke solutions for clients. The biggest project I created during my time there was to create a database application. I made it to help healthcare professionals narrow down potential wheelchair options for clients. This was mainly to reduce time, paperwork and needless re-scheduling for simple appointments. A healthcare professional could type in measurements from a client into the search function of my database and then narrow down the potential wheelchairs and their potential configurations, reducing the amount of calls to our office for questions.

Moving forward, I am setting my sights on a career in Software Development, either working on my practical skills in a company. Or learning theoretical skills in further education.
